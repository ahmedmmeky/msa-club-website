Discover PSU MSA

Asalamu Alaykum! WE ARE Penn State MSA! We strive to bring unity for Muslims of Penn State while developing a sense of brotherhood, and sisterhood within the Penn State community. Our vision is to be the family and home for all Penn State Muslims. We strive to build meaningful and life-long relationships with our members and the overarching PSU community!
